I have other questions about registering to vote in California. Who can I ask for help?

    Call the California Secretary of State’s toll-free voter hotline at 1-800-345-VOTE (English), 1-800-232-VOTA (Spanish), 1-800-339-2857 (Chinese), or 1-800-833-8683 (TTY/TDD);[260] OR

LEARN HOW TO FIND YOUR COUNTY ELECTIONS OFFICE HERE!

    Contact your county elections office — This office maintains your voter registration record (if you have one).[261] To locate your local county elections office, visit the California Secretary of State’s website at: http://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voting-resources/find-your-polling-place/. There you will see an alphabetical directory of county elections offices. If you don’t know your county, you can call the voter hotline numbers above, or look it up online by typing in your ZIP code at http://quickfacts.census.gov/cgi-bin/qfd/lookup.
