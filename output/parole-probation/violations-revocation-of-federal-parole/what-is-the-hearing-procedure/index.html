What is the hearing procedure?

What is the hearing procedure?

The purpose is to determine whether you have violated the conditions of your release and, if so, whether your parole or mandatory release should be (1) revoked (taken away) or (2) reinstated (where you continue on parole as you were).[982] To get more information about how to prepare for the hearing, please see the Appendix KK, PG. 367
