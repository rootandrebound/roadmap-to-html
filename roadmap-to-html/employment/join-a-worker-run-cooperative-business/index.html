Join a worker-run cooperative business

A worker cooperative is a business that is owned and controlled by its workers. In other words, the workers are both the employees AND the employer. Usually, workers run the day-to-day operations of the business‚Äîsuch as making business decisions, assigning tasks to each person, etc., as well as the business‚Äôs finances.
