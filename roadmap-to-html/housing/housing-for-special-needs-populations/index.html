Housing for Special Needs & Populations

This section provides a brief overview of housing resources for people in reentry with special needs and who might qualify for special programs, including:

    Women & Children (PG. 343)
    Domestic Violence Survivors (PG. 344)
    Seniors/ Elders (PG. 346)
    Veterans (PG. 346)
    People Recovering from Substance Abuse/ Addiction (PG. 347)
    290 Sex Offender Registrants (PG. 348)
