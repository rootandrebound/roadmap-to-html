What type of ID can I get if I am undocumented?

You may be able to get one of these limited forms of ID, which can be used for some but not all purposes:Consular Identification Cards (CIDs): Some governments issue CID cards to identify their citizens who are living in foreign countries. CID cards can be issued to people who are undocumented or documented in the foreign county. See Appendix¬†H, PG.¬†115, for a list of countries that issue CIDs. If you are a citizen of one of these countries, visit the nearest consulate to obtain your CID card. In the U.S., CID cards can be a helpful ID document as you try to get a Driver License, open a bank account, show proof of identity to police, and access other services. However, CIDs do not grant you legal presence in the U.S. or other privileges.[197] Municipal IDs: A handful of California cities have begun issuing municipal (‚Äúcity‚Äù) ID cards for their residents. Additionally, these forms of ID do not require proof of citizenship or legal presence of any kind.
