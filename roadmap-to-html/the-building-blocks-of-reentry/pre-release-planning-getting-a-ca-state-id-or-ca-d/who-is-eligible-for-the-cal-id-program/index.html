Who is eligible for the CAL-ID program?

Under the expanded CAL-ID program, you are eligible for a CA State ID if:

    You have a set release date AND you are 120 - 210 days (approximately 4-7 months) away from that release date;
    You previously had a CA State ID or CA Driver License;
    Your previous CA State ID or Driver License was issued in the past 10 years;
    You don‚Äôt owe any DMV fees for your previous CA State ID or Driver License;
    You have a photo on file with the DMV that is no more than 10 years old;
    You don‚Äôt have any active felony holds, warrants, or detainers that could cause you to go back to prison or jail after your release;

    You can provide an address where you will live after your release (Note: this is the address that will be printed on the CA State ID card, not your prison address); AND
